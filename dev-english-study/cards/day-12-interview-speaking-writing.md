# Day 12：面试 / 对话 / 书面表达

## 1. From my experience...
- 中文：根据我的经验……
- 高频短句：From my experience, clear API contracts reduce communication costs.
- 记忆提示：表达观点时很好用。

## 2. One of the biggest challenges was...
- 中文：最大的挑战之一是……
- 高频短句：One of the biggest challenges was reducing latency under heavy load.
- 记忆提示：面试讲项目高频模板。

## 3. We solved this by...
- 中文：我们通过……解决了这个问题
- 高频短句：We solved this by adding caching and optimizing queries.
- 记忆提示：适合 STAR 回答。

## 4. The result was...
- 中文：结果是……
- 高频短句：The result was a 40 percent reduction in response time.
- 记忆提示：项目成果表达。

## 5. I want to clarify the expected behavior.
- 中文：我想先澄清预期行为。
- 高频短句：I want to clarify the expected behavior before implementation.
- 记忆提示：需求不清时特别好用。

## 6. I agree with the direction, but...
- 中文：我认同这个方向，但是……
- 高频短句：I agree with the direction, but I am concerned about the migration cost.
- 记忆提示：专业表达不同意见。

## 7. Thanks for pointing that out.
- 中文：感谢你指出这一点。
- 高频短句：Thanks for pointing that out. I will update the implementation.
- 记忆提示：PR 评论回复好用。

## 8. We are still investigating the root cause.
- 中文：我们还在排查根因。
- 高频短句：We are still investigating the root cause.
- 记忆提示：线上故障同步高频句。

## 9. Please correct me if I am wrong.
- 中文：如果我理解错了请纠正我。
- 高频短句：Please correct me if I am wrong, but this field seems redundant.
- 记忆提示：礼貌但专业。

## 10. I will follow up on this.
- 中文：我会继续跟进这件事。
- 高频短句：I will follow up on this after the release.
- 记忆提示：结尾承诺常用。
