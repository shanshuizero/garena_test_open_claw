# Anki Day 30：综合复习 2

## 卡片格式
Front｜Back

1. **Front**: Please correct me if I am wrong.
   **Back**: 如果我理解错了请纠正我。｜Please correct me if I am wrong, but this field seems redundant.｜提示：礼貌表达

2. **Front**: I will follow up on this.
   **Back**: 我会继续跟进这件事。｜I will follow up on this after the release.｜提示：结尾承诺

3. **Front**: We solved this by...
   **Back**: 我们通过……解决了这个问题。｜We solved this by adding caching and optimizing queries.｜提示：项目表达

4. **Front**: The result was...
   **Back**: 结果是……｜The result was a 40 percent reduction in response time.｜提示：结果表达

5. **Front**: From my experience...
   **Back**: 根据我的经验……｜From my experience, clear API contracts reduce communication costs.｜提示：观点表达
