# Anki Day 27：面试：自我介绍 / 优势 / 项目挑战

## 卡片格式
Front｜Back

1. **Front**: Tell me about yourself.
   **Back**: 请介绍一下你自己。｜I am a software engineer with experience in backend development.｜提示：面试题

2. **Front**: One of my biggest strengths is...
   **Back**: 我最大的优势之一是……｜One of my biggest strengths is breaking down complex problems.｜提示：面试题

3. **Front**: One of the most challenging issues I faced was...
   **Back**: 我遇到过最有挑战的问题之一是……｜One of the most challenging issues I faced was a latency spike.｜提示：项目挑战

4. **Front**: We first checked the metrics and logs.
   **Back**: 我们先看了指标和日志。｜We first checked the metrics and logs.｜提示：排障表达

5. **Front**: This significantly improved response time.
   **Back**: 这显著提升了响应时间。｜This significantly improved response time.｜提示：结果表达
