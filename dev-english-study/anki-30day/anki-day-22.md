# Anki Day 22：基础工作对话

## 卡片格式
Front｜Back

1. **Front**: What are you working on today?
   **Back**: 你今天在做什么？｜What are you working on today?｜提示：站会高频句

2. **Front**: Do you have any blockers?
   **Back**: 你有阻塞吗？｜Do you have any blockers?｜提示：站会高频句

3. **Front**: I am currently blocked by...
   **Back**: 我目前被……卡住了｜I am currently blocked by a permission issue.｜提示：汇报阻塞

4. **Front**: Please let me know once it is fixed.
   **Back**: 修好后请告诉我｜Please let me know once it is fixed.｜提示：联调常用

5. **Front**: Could you review this pull request?
   **Back**: 你能看一下这个 PR 吗？｜Could you review this pull request?｜提示：协作高频
