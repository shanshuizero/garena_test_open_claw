综合复习 2

支持卡片式 Anki 浏览与晨读口语切换。

Anki 抽卡

Please correct me if I am wrong.
如果我理解错了请纠正我。｜Please correct me if I am wrong, but this field seems redundant.｜提示：礼貌表达
I will follow up on this.
我会继续跟进这件事。｜I will follow up on this after the release.｜提示：结尾承诺
We solved this by...
我们通过……解决了这个问题。｜We solved this by adding caching and optimizing queries.｜提示：项目表达
The result was...
结果是……｜The result was a 40 percent reduction in response time.｜提示：结果表达
From my experience...
根据我的经验……｜From my experience, clear API contracts reduce communication costs.｜提示：观点表达

晨读口语

Please correct me if I am wrong, but this field seems redundant.
中文：如果我理解错了请纠正我。
提示：礼貌表达
I will follow up on this after the release.
中文：我会继续跟进这件事。
提示：结尾承诺
We solved this by adding caching and optimizing queries.
中文：我们通过……解决了这个问题。
提示：项目表达
The result was a 40 percent reduction in response time.
中文：结果是……
提示：结果表达
From my experience, clear API contracts reduce communication costs.
中文：根据我的经验……
提示：观点表达
建议：先慢读 2 遍，再正常语速读 3 遍，最后脱稿复述其中 2 句。
